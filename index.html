Our Story

Bridging Research & Real-World Impact

Bradley Research was founded with a singular purpose: to make rigorous, contextually relevant research accessible to healthcare organisations throughout Africa. We recognised a critical gap between the quality of global research and its application in African healthcare systems.

Our multidisciplinary team — comprising researchers, clinicians, data analysts, and strategy consultants — brings together academic excellence and hands-on field experience to deliver research that is immediately actionable.

From Johannesburg to Eswatini, our work has shaped healthcare policy, improved patient safety protocols, and equipped professionals with the tools they need to deliver better care.
